
  ☢  Finnish Radiation Monitor
  Dose rate data from STUK / FMI OpenData · 253 stations · 10-min averages

Usage:

    $ curl radiation.wttr.in           # your location
    $ curl radiation.wttr.in/Helsinki  # by municipality name
    $ curl radiation.wttr.in/Nuijamaa

Dose rate levels:

    normal    < 0.40 µSv/h
    elevated  0.40 – 1.00 µSv/h
    HIGH      > 1.00 µSv/h

    (typical Finnish background radiation: 0.04 – 0.30 µSv/h)

Reference doses:

    Chest X-ray              ≈ 0.10 µSv
    Helsinki–London flight   ≈ 20   µSv
    Annual limit (public)    ≈ 1 000 µSv/year
